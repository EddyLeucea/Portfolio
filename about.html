Why I Chose Application & AI Development

I chose this field because I believe AI and intelligent systems will play a major role in the future of technology, business, and everyday life. That long-term impact motivates me, but what keeps me genuinely interested is the technology itself. I enjoy learning how AI tools, LLMs, and modern software systems work behind the scenes, and I want to build the technical foundation needed to contribute to that future in a meaningful way.
